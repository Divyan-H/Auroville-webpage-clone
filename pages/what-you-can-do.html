What You Can Do

Auroville is a live-in research campus, not a tourist destination. We welcome people who are keen on exploring solutions to the global challenges humanity is facing. You can visit, stay, volunteer, attend workshops and classes, enrol in our educational programmes, buy high-quality products made in Auroville, partner with Auroville units, become a donor or join Auroville as a member.
